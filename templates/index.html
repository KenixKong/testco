About

SY Kong & Co. (NF 2351) is an accounting firm located at Tapah, Perak. The firm started with Kong Accounting & General Services (江会计事务所) which also located at the same office and serve different type of business for more than 30 years.

With diverse experience in business over the years, we able to provide you with our professional advice. Our firm and team are providing services such as business consultancy, accounting support and bookkeeping services, business consultancy, accounting software, tax consultancy service and many more.

What we do

Our firm is providing services such as business consultancy, accounting and bookkeeping services, business consultancy, accounting software, tax consultancy service, company registration, outsource payroll service and other business support services.

Accounting support and bookkeeping services

We offer a wide range of accounting support for small to medium-sized enterprises (SME). We assist in keeping detailed records, keep your business transaction recorded in proper manner and updated monthly, quarterly, or yearly.

Management services / Outsourcing payroll service

Our team provide management services such as renewal of business license, outsource payroll support service. We also assist in prepare payroll, submission of statutory payment and other payment. Statutory payment such as EPF, SOCSO, EIS, PCB, HRDF submission.

Tax consultancy service

We assist in prepare of tax computation from individual to company level.

Business consultancy

Our team assist in company registration and business consultancy accordance to your business nature.
